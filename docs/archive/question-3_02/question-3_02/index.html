Question 02 / Spørsmål 02

March 10, 2022   

Welcome to the hillside! Here we have a question about food.

We throw a lot of food in Norway. How many people could we have fed if we halved the food waste in the country?

- About 500 000 people, which is more than the total population of Trøndelag per 2020.
- About 200 000 people, which is more than the total population of Trondheim per 2020.
- About 370 000 people, which is about the population of Innlandet County per 2021.
- About 1 000 000 people, which is about the population of Oslo per 2021.
