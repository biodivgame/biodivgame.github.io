Map and information / Kart og informasjon

April 1, 2022   

Welcome to our biodiversity game! It is stationed in and around the Arboretum. The game consists of following hints and QR-codes through a route, answering questions about species, biodiversity and connected topics. If you want to start, find the stake near the place where the insects can check in over winter!

The game is made by four students at NTNU in collaboration with the botanical garden and is meant to be an offer to international visitors.

For more/official info about the park, take a look at the official web page.
